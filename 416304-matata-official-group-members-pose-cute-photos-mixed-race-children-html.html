    On June 20, 2021, members of Norway-based Kenyan music group Matata introduced their adorable children via social media.

    While celebrating Father’s Day, the five-member group revealed that three of them are fathers to children sired with Caucasian women.

    Matata members who are fathers are Freddy Milanya, Richard Mathu and Marcus Ojiambo.

    The group celebrated the three dads by writing:

    “Happy Father’s day. Celebrating the Matata fathers.”

    The post was accompanied by three photos of the award-winning musicians with their kids.

    The first photo depicted Marcus with his cute little daughter sitting at his feet. In the second photo, Richie and his son posed with the peace sign.
