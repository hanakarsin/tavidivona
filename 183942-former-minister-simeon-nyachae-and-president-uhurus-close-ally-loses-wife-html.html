    Ex-minister Simeon Nyachae loses wife

    -Former minister and FORD People party leader Simeon Nyachae's wife is dead - Nyachae is a key ally of President Uhuru Kenyatta; his son - Charles - is said to be closely working with Jubilee with eyes on the Kisii gubernatorial seat

    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    - Former minister and FORD People party leader Simeon Nyachae's wife is dead

    - Nyachae is a key ally of President Uhuru Kenyatta; his son - Charles - is said to be closely working with Jubilee with eyes on the Kisii gubernatorial seat

    Former minister Simeon Nyachae has lost his wife.

    Simeon Nyachae's wife, Esther Kerubo Nyachae, passed on on Tuesday, August 30.

    Kisii Governor James Ongwae was among the first leaders  and locals to send their condolences to the family.

    Details about the cause of her death and burial arrangements are yet to be made public.

    Simeon Nyachae is a close political ally to President Uhuru Kenyatta and arguably a key Jubilee point man in Kisii region.

    He has not been so active in politics but his son - Charles Nyachae - has publicly endorsed Uhuru's 2017 reelection bid.

    Charles has been working closely with Jubilee ever since his term as the chairman of the Constitution Implementation Commission ended. He is reportedly eyeing the Kisii gubernatorial seat in 2017.

    The close relationship with the ruling alliance saw the family reap big in terms of state appointments. In April 2015, Uhuru was criticised for appointing five members of Nyachae's family to plum government positions.

    Simeon Nyachae's daughter-in-law Judy got appointed to the Export Processing Zone board. His son, Kenneth Bitange Nyachae, got a job at the Kenya Civil Aviation Authority.
