    Many people, specifically artistes love creating stronger bases in their home areas before breaking borders.

    For instance, it is quite a gamble to try out new markets but several artistes have been trying their hands at it, with Nigerian singer Acerbergtm being the latest one to join the bandwagon.

    TUKO.co.ke has learnt Acerbergtm, born Ikechukwu Okoronkwo, will be dropping his debut Extended Play(EP) dubbed Far From Home on Friday, April 16, 2021.

    Unlike most musicians who would do the same in their homes, Acerbergtm will be launching his EP in Nairobi, Kenya, way far from his home.

    To warm up his fans, the singer will first drop his first single named Danca on Friday, March 26, still in Nairobi.

    TUKO.co.ke has learnt that Acerbergtm will not only release his works and fly back to Nigeria but also do a media tour across East Africa.

    Speaking about his Kenyan tour, the artiste said he also intends to popularise his EP and work on several collabos with East African artistes.

    ''I can not wait to share my gift with my Kenyan and East African fans,'' Acerbergtm said."Growing up as a kid, music turned out to be my only escape from peer pressure and other vices which kids my age were involved in such as smoking and street fighting; I got a strong sense of peace and satisfaction every time I heard my favourite songs growing up. My interest grew even more as I got older, I wanted to write, sing, rap, perform just like my favourite artists on TV" he added.

    Acerbergtm made a debut in the music industry in 2020 with the release of his first single Heartbreak.

    The single has garnered over 7 million streams across all digital platforms and 764,000 views on YouTube.

    His latest body of work - The Far from Home EP consists of 6 tracks and showcases his unique talent of fusing different genres.

    TUKO.co.ke understands Acerbgtm is signed to Sir Justine World Entertainment Ltd.
