    Singer Jennifer Lopez and fiance Alex Rodriguez have called off their engagement after being together for close to four years.

    The power couple had planned to do a grand wedding in 2020 but had to cancel it twice due to the coronavirus pandemic.

    E! News and The People Magazine outlets were the first to report about the break up with sources claiming that the two had a looming split, which was inevitable.

    ''BREAKING! After two years, Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez have called off their engagement,'' posted E! News.

    There were also speculations that Rodriguez cheated on the talented singer but it was unclear if that played a role in their separation.

    However, since the pandemic started in 2020, the two moved in together.

    Lopez brought with her, her 13-year-old twin sons while Rodriguez had his two daughters from his previous marriage.

    On Friday, March 12, the baseball player posted a picture of himself enjoying some alone time on a yacht.

    Other reports also claim that the power couple had entangled their businesses ever since they started dating and that was one of the reasons they found it so hard to part ways initially.
