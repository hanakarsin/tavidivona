    Ebuka Obi-Uchendu Reacts To Accusation Of Joining Illuminati

    The Illuminati is allegedly a secret organization for high personalities that supposedly has the power to influence world affairs, control the media, and sway governments. It is rumoured that some of the most famous celebrities around the world are a part of the secret society.

    25 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Former Big Brother Naija Housemate cum Media personality, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu, has reacted to the accusation of joining the Illuminati after his photos with popular billionaire, Bill Gates emerged online.

    What Is Illuminati?

    The Illuminati is allegedly a secret organization for high personalities that supposedly has the power to influence world affairs, control the media, and sway governments. It is rumoured that some of the most famous celebrities around the world are a part of the secret society.

    Naija News recall that Ebuka had earlier last week, taken to his Instagram page to share a series of photos he took with Microsoft founder, his ex-wife, Melinda and renowned writer, Chimamanda Adiche in New York, where he hosted the Goalkeepers 2022 awards.

    One of the photos shared by Ebuka elicited suspicion from an IG user with the handle @Phemolino1313, claiming that the media personality had joined Illuminati.

    The Instagram user wrote: “Ebuka we already know you just got signed into the great Illuminati controlled by the likes of Bill Gates so no need giving us the handling before we affirm it… Just know fame doesn’t last long… 2pac got signed but got dropped, Mj got signed but got dropped and many more.”

    However, Ebuka reacted by questioning the IG user why he hadn’t received his $100 million welcome alert if he had truly joined the secret society.

    He wrote: “You mean I joined the Illuminati and I still haven’t received my $100m welcome alert yet?”
