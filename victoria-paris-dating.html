    Few people on TikTok are as polarizing as Victoria Paris. Whether you love her or hate her, though, plenty of people are definitely curious about who she's dating at any given moment. Victoria tends to date women, but many want to know exactly who she's dating now, and what she's said in public about them.

    Who is TikTok star Victoria Paris dating?

    In the past, Victoria has taken steps to keep her love life as under wraps as possible. Though she's a public figure with a considerable online following, she made several efforts to protect the privacy of the romantic partners she had in the past who didn't want to be in the spotlight as much as her.

    That being said, Victoria did share some minor details about her previous relationship.

    Article continues below advertisement

    In a July 2022 interview with PEOPLE, Victoria revealed that she had started dating a scientist at the time. Reportedly, her girlfriend at the time was not on TikTok, but had earned her PhD. The influencer even expressed her intentions to "go fully public" with their relationship so that the two women could "build a solid foundation for [their] relationship."

    According to Victoria, the two of them had met in Los Angeles after connecting on BeReal.

    As far as Victorians can tell, however, their relationship must have fizzled out, especially now that Victoria has been able to achieve that openness with another woman in her life as of this year.

    Article continues below advertisement

    Victoria Paris is currently dating someone new.

    In early September 2023, Victoria posted a TikTok in which she shared the story of meeting her current girlfriend, Selma Kaci (@selmakacisebbaccgh). Based on the video, we can already tell that these two are way more comfortable being open about their relationship on social media, especially considering that Selma is more active on TikTok.

    Article continues below advertisement

    According to her own TikTok profile, which currently has over 32,000 followers, Selma is a travel and fashion influencer who posts plenty of videos depicting her visits to exotic locations. It's no wonder that she and Victoria eventually connected.

    As Victoria remembers it, the two of them connected on TikTok after Victorians would tag her in Selma's videos claiming that they looked remarkably similar to each other. They eventually slid into each other's DMs and subsequently met with each other in person while in Paris. In fact, they just happened to bump into each other outside of a "matcha place."

    Article continues below advertisement

    The two of them ended up bouncing around Europe on a bit of a holiday before meeting up in Marseilles. As if to say "the rest is history," the video ends with Victoria and Selma sharing a romantically awkward kiss.

    There you have it! Not only is Victoria officially dating, but she's been able to share more details about her relationship than she might have done previously. And what better way to kick off a romance than in one of the most romantic cities in the world?
