    03 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Director General of Tinubu/Shettima Presidential Campaign Council and Governor of Plateau State, Simon Lalong, has boasted that the presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC) will emerge victorious come 2023.

    Lalong stated this while declaring today that Jos is ready for the APC presidential campaign flag-off scheduled for Tuesday, November 15, 2022.

    The governor who noted that preparation has been concluded to host President Muhammadu Buhari and the APC presidential candidate, Asiwaju Bola Tinubu and his running mate, Kashim Shettima in the state, said every presidential candidate declared in the state always wins the election.

    Naija News understands that Lalong said this in an interview with journalists on Monday after the inspection of the Rwang Pam Township Stadium Jos, the venue of the APC presidential campaign flag-off.

    He expressed satisfaction with the level of preparedness and said history is about to be made as all presidential campaign that was flagged off in Jos in the past have always translated to victory.

    The APC chieftain said: “History is about to repeat itself because Jos used to be the only comfortable place for political campaigns until the construction of Eagle Square Abuja. But we are now returning back to Jos again because of the weather, hospitality and warm reception.

    ”Mr President gladly accepted the choice of Jos and our Presidential candidate is also very comfortable with coming to Jos we have prepared very well because we have a competent person, the Deputy Speaker, House of Representatives, Rt. Hon. Ahmed Idris Wase as Chairman of the Local Organizing Committee and my Deputy Governor, Prof. Sonni Tyoden.

    “I will say we have made a very remarkable improvement in the preparation and by the grace of God we are going to have a very peaceful exercise.”

    Long added that the cost of APC winning the presidential election is very clear, adding that, “God is making the history himself that everybody that is declared here will become the President of Nigeria.

    “Abiola was here and became President of Nigeria, Obasanjo came here and became President of Nigeria and we went to Lagos where President Buhari was produced and we want Asiwaju Bola Tinubu to come to Jos and become the President of Nigeria”.

    On his part, the Deputy Speaker House of Representatives, Rt. Hon. Ahmed Idris Wase, Chairman Local Organizing Committee, said security has been beefed up for all dignitaries who are coming for the campaign flag-off.

    “We are ready to host the presidential campaign flag-off, we are going to have the most secured environment and we are making frantic efforts to ensure that the environment is suitable to everybody who is coming; we know that our security is prepared to respond to every situation,” he stated.
