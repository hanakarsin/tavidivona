    22 Apr 2024, 00:00

    - Joshua is Fani-Kayode's first son

    - Joshua was born on Monday, February, 1

    - Joshua has five names including his father's first name

    When Legit.ng called the former minister of aviation, Femi Fani-Kayode, to confirm the birth of his first son and he said his son is “beautiful”, we did not understand what he meant.

    But looking at these photos, you will agree with the former minister that Fani-kayode (junior) is indeed beautiful.

    Born on Monday, February, 1, Joshua (the Lord saves), Oluwafemi (the beloved of the Lord), Emmanuel (the Lord is with us), Lotanna (remembering the Father) Aragorn (the kingly warrior) Fani-Kayode was christened today, February 8.

    His christening which happened seven days after his birth was a quiet one with only close family members and officiating ministers present, a source close to the family told Legit.ng.

    The former minister has two children, girls (Folake, a UK-based lawyer, and Remi, who is based in Accra, Ghana) before the arrival of Joshua.

    READ ALSO: EXCLUSIVE: At last! Fani-Kayode begets a son

    Joshua who took his mother’s light skinned complexion was born to the family of Fani-Kayode and Precious Chikwendu, a former Miss Valentine and Miss United Nations World 2014.

    Precious, is a notable Nollywood actress and model; she is popularly known as Snow White.

    Sources close to to the family told Legit.ng that the former minister and his partner, Precious are very excited over the birth of the child.
