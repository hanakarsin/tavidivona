    3. Be consistent

    Nobody would prefer to follow a profile that does not have any regularity in its content or its posts. Imagine yourself following a celebrity. You are following him/her because you are interested in their posts and updates. How would you start to feel if you saw their post once in a month? It would soon feel frustrating. Try to make regular posts; in that way, your followers will stick to you for your activity. Preferably, schedule your posts so that your followers know what to look for and when. Use the story option to provide a pre-update of your upcoming post.

    4. Have a theme

    Organized and symmetrical things are preferable over untidy and unpolished stuff. It is the same with this photo-sharing platform. Do not go on to be a jack of all trades; rather, try to opt for one genre or topic that will be the base of your posts. Suppose you are a fitness enthusiast; post about gyms and workout routines etc. Talk about a certain topic and associate your posts with it. It not only helps in the consistency bit but also makes you reach out to a specific audience. Building an organic audience generates more response.

    Link your profile to other social sites. Participate in conversations in different groups or pages.
