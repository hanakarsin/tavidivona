    A Michigan woman in the US hit a very huge jackpot when she was matched with billionaire Bill Gates as her secret Santa.

    Shelby got 81 pounds worth of books, treats and other amazing things plus a donation made to the American Heart Association in her mother’s name, Market Watch reports.

    The 33-year-old woman said that she had always thought it would really be great to be matched with someone as big as Bill Gates but never knew it would be this cool.

    “I always thought it would be super cool to be matched with him someday, but I never really would have expected this to happen to me,” she said.

    Her profile shows she is a fan of movies like Harry Porter and Star Wars. Little wonder the gifts she got included some of the things she loves most.

    On her view of the gift program, she said she would keep participating in it as it brings a whole lot of joy.

    “I am definitely gonna keep participating in Reddit Gifts. It brings me a lot of joy. I cannot wait to continue, and to spoil more people with presents, and pass it forward,” she said.
