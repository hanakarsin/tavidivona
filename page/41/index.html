A host of dignitaries were in attendance on Tuesday in Abuja for the launch of a book in honour of former President Muhammadu Buhari. Some of the dignitaries who attended the book launch which held at the conference Hall of the Transcorp Hilton hotel in Abuja include President Tinubu, former Head of State, Yakubu Gowon, former Vice President, Yemi Osinbajo, former First Lady, Aisha Buhari, the Senate President, Senator Godswill Akpabio, Speaker of the House of Representatives, Tajudeen Abbas, among several others.
