Yoruba Nation Agitator, Sunday Igboho Set To Return To Nigeria

28 Jun 2024, 00:00

A Yoruba nation agitator, Sunday Adeyemo, popularly known as Sunday Igboho, is planning to return to Nigeria, according to his lawyer, Mr. Pelumi Olajengbesi. In 2021, Igboho left the country following a raid on his Ibadan home by the Department of State Services (DSS). The raid resulted in the deaths of two people and led to the arrest of around 13 others. The DSS confirmed they had conducted the operation due to intelligence reports suggesting that Igboho was amassing weapons in his home.
