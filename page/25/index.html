NBA Has Provided 427 Lawyers ToProsecute 215 Electoral Offenders For Free

22 Jul 2024, 00:00

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has disclosed that the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) has offered it 427 lawyers to prosecute 215 electoral offenders from the 2023 general elections. Naija News gathered that the Commission’s Chairman, Prof Mahmood Yakubu disclosed this on Tuesday during a meeting with the state Resident Electoral Commissioners (RECs) to review the general elections. The INEC boss said the Nigerian police force presented to the commission 215 people for various electoral infractions, and the NBA is helping in prosecuting the culprits by providing the services of 427 lawyers for free to INEC.
