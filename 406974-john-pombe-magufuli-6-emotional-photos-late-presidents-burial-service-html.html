    Protocols to be followed during Magufuli's burial

    The CCM leader would be laid to rest in a private function as planned by the government.

    Read also

    Given the provisions in the Tanzanian law that specifies how the head of state should be buried, Magufuli's final journey will observe a set of protocols.

    As stipulated in the National Leaders Funeral Acts of 2006, four important protocols will be observed during the sendoff.

    Before Magufuli's interment, a special parade shall be mounted by the Tanzania People's Defence Force (TPDF) at the place of his burial, which the Defence Forces will organise with the assistance of the National Funeral committee.

    At the burial site, the TPDF soldiers will fire a 21 gun salute, regarded as the departed leader's highest honour.

    Thereafter, the procedural matters regarding the parade's conduct will be prescribed by the Minister responsible for defence.

    Throughout the four protocols, Magufuli's bier will be covered by the national flag.
