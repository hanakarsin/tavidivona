    This Baltimore-based tattoo artist has felt a "super strong connection" to Harry Potter since she was a kid. She owns Dark Arts Tattoo Studio, which was inspired by the Potter series. One of Potter's classes at Hogwarts was called "Defence Against the Dark Arts," Graves says the three most-requested tattoos revolve around Harry Potter, anim√©, and Pokemon. She opened Dark Arts a month before the pandemic, and the lockdown, hit, which is when she started posting to TikTok, where she now has 1.2M followers, and another 152K on Instagram. 

    Before Fame

    She spent five years tattooing at Spill the Ink in nearby Edgewood before opening Dark Arts.

    Trivia

    Family Life

    She has a husband and a daughter.

    Associated With
