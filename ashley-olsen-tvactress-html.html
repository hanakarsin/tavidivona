    Became a TV star portraying Michelle Tanner at nine months old on the series Full House, sharing the role with her fraternal twin sister Mary-Kate Olsen. The twins starred in a number of films in the '90s and also went on to run their own fashion brands. They founded the luxury fashion brand The Row in 2006. 

    Before Fame

    She and Mary-Kate were born in Sherman Oaks, California to Jarnette and David Olsen.

    Trivia

    Among the many movies she and Mary-Kate star in as young actresses are It Takes Two, Billboard Dad and Passport to Paris.

    Family Life

    She has a brother named Trent. Her mom is named Jarnette Jones. She has a half sister named Courtney Taylor Olsen. She dated director Bennett Miller in 2014. In 2017, she began dating painter Louis Eisner. The couple married in January 2023. She gave birth to their first child together later that year. 

    Associated With

    Her younger sister Elizabeth Olsen starred in multiple Marvel movies and television shows, such as WandaVision, as The Scarlet Witch.
