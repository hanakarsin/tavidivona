    Kenyan blogger behind bars over his social media post

    - Police are holding a political blogger who has been accused of spreading hatred messages on social media - The blogger, Paul Odhiambo was arrested on Tuesday, July 4 2017 and locked up at the Busia Police Station for questioning

    21 Apr 2024, 00:00

    - Police are holding a political blogger who has been accused of spreading hatred messages on social media

    - The blogger, Paul Odhiambo was arrested on Tuesday, July 4 2017 and locked up at the Busia Police Station for questioning

    A Kenyan blogger is currently cooling his heels in a police cell after he was arrested for publishing messages said to be offensive on his social media page.

    TUKO.co.ke has learnt that the Busia-based blogger who has been identified as Paul Odhiambo alias Amemba was arrested on Tuesday, July 4 2017, and locked up at the Busia Police Station for further questioning.

    Confirming the arrest, Busia OCPD Masai Makau said Odhiambo was arrested following complaints from members of the public, who accused him of spreading hatred messages through his Facebook page and WhatsApp.
