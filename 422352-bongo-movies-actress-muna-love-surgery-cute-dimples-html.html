    Some are even willing to go under the knife to change a thing or two in their bodies just but to acquire their ''perfect'' forms.

    A case in point is a Bongo Movies actress Muna Love who recently went through cosmetic surgery to get some cute dimples.

    Muna Love broke the news that she had gone for the surgery through her official Instagram account.
