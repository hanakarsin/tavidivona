    2022/23 UEFA Champions League Group Stage Draw

    Naija News reports that the UEFA Champions League group stage draw was made on Thursday, August 25 at 18:00 local time in Istanbul, Turkey. English Premier League side, Liverpool was drawn in Group A with Ajax, Napoli and Rangers.

    14 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Draws have been made for the 2022/23 Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) champions league season.

    Naija News reports that the UEFA Champions League group stage draw was made on Thursday, August 25 at 18:00 local time in Istanbul, Turkey.

    English Premier League side, Liverpool was drawn in Group A with Ajax, Napoli and Rangers.

    Portuguese professional football club, FC Porto led Group B which has Atletico Madrid, Leverkusen and Club Bruge, while Bundesliga side, Bayern Munich was drawn in Group C alongside Barcelona, Inter Milan and Viktoria Plzen.
