    Gospel singer Daddy Owen has been hitting the headlines for a while now and this time round, the minister questioned himself after keenly listening to his hit song Vanity.

    Read also

    The Mbona hit maker has been battling depression and recently made his situation public after three years of dealing with it silently.

    His song Vanity was written by fellow gospel artiste Pitson. He tagged him on Instagram asking him what was on his mind when he penned down that song for him.

    ''But @therealpitson seriously why did you write this song for me? I have gone through the lyrics and asked myself so many questions. #Ifyouknowyouknow.''

    The 40-year-old is also dealing with separation from his wife who is alleged to have left him for a richer man.

    Earlier, TUKO.co.ke reported on the singer's marital woes that left majority of Kenyans thinking he was just pulling a stunt online.

    His celebrity wife Farida allegedly left him for a Gilgil tycoon and the two were said to be planning to spend the rest of their lives together.

    The news left many Kenyans shocked. Some, however, believed that the allegations were part of a publicity stunt to promote an upcoming project.

    But, Owen later refuted those claims and is now coming to terms with the end of his marriage.

    Back to his latest post, it not very clear why the Kazi Ya Msalaba singer reflected on his hit song and why this particular song out of the many he has released.
