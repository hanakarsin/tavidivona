    Have you lost your JAMB registration number and do not know what to do? Joint Admission and Matriculation Registration Number is the only thing that matters for young Nigerians who wants to get admission into a higher institution. If you have lost it, then you will want to retrieve it. How to retrieve JAMB registration number? Follow this complete guideline to find the answer to your question!

    How to retrieve JAMB registration number?

    I have lost my JAMB registration number – what should I do? How to check JAMB registration number? First check for your JAMB registration number by considering the last place you saw it or used it. You might have written it somewhere on a paper or a word document on your computer. If you have problems in finding it and you've looked everywhere, then you have two options to retrieve it.

    How to get JAMB registration number – Option number 1

    If you have lost your JAMB registration number, then you can get it back with the validation of the PIN, Serial Number, first name, family name, date of birth and state of origin. How to do that? Just follow these simple steps!

    Step 1

    Get the following necessities to retrieve your JAMB registration number:

    • Serial Number
    • PIN

    Step 2

    Go to the official JAMB registration number portal

    Step 3

    When the web page is loaded, you will see two blank boxes. Write in your Serial number and PIN into this two blank boxes.

    Step 4

    When you have entered your Serial number and PIN – click the submit button, and you will get all the details displayed on your screen. Here you will get your JAMB registration number. This is also possible for JAMB registration number for NYSC.

    Find JAMB registration number – Option number 2

    Visit your nearest JAMB registration centre. They keep records of all registered candidates. You just need to present your serial number, PIN or ID to retrieve your JAMB registration number. They can also provide you official information about the regularization of JAMB registration number.

    How to validate JAMB registration number?

    If you followed all options mentioned above - then you must be relieved with your retrieval of your JAMB registration number! Now you know how to retrieve JAMB registration number. It is nothing difficult. Still, it`s advisable not to lose your registration number. Note, that a JAMB centre may decide to charge a fee if they have to find it for you.
